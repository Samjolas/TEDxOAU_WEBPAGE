Welcome
To TEDxOAU

In the spirit of ideas worth spreading, TEDx is a program of local, self-organized events that bring people together to share a TED-like experience. At a TEDx event, TED Talks video and live speakers combine to spark deep discussion and connection. in summary we’re TEDX by the greatest, for the greatest

The Obafemi Awolowo University community has always been home to amazing conversations, tenacious humans, radical innovations, a little too many tote bags, and—of course—its iconic bats. Now, it’s going to be a hotspot for ideas worth spreading by humans worth listening to on grounds we’ve come to love. And whether you identify as an Awo boy or a Maintenance chick, a fresher or a stallite, a T-shirt fanatic or a fashion icon, an environmentally-conscious trekker or a bike addict, a shana or a… well, whatever—there’s a TEDxOAU Talk that’s just right for you. We’d love for you to join us.

Why Attend?

Meet

TED conferences are worldwide that open a world of opportunity to attendees, speakers, and sponsors. They provide platforms to share ideas, engage in exciting conversations, and network with amazing humans.

Connect

And like thousands of other TED and TEDx events that have held in the past, TEDxOAU holds the same promise of high-quality conversations and connections. But, of course, the conference will feature all the energy you’ve come to expect from the largest academic community in the ancient city of Ile-Ife.

Talk

Expect extraordinary talks from remarkable speakers on diverse topics, an opportunity to make impactful connections with progressive peers, as well as a chance to be a part of one of the biggest conferences on earth.
